Educational Tour

Tour Highlights

Day 1

After landing at Mactan Airport from Davao International Airport, we boarded a bus and toured Mactan, where we learned about the historic battle between Lapu-Lapu and Magellan. We then went to Vikings for lunch, giving us time to rest and bond as a group. After lunch, we proceeded to UP Cebu to attend a scheduled meeting with one of the companies. Following the meeting, we explored Cebu City, visiting the Cebu City Hall area and Magellan’s Cross. We also entered the Basilica Minore del Sto. Niño de Cebu, a significant religious and cultural landmark. Later, we bought pasalubong from nearby shops around City Hall, visited Parkmall, and ended the day by checking in at Bai Hotel to rest for the night.

Day 2

Day 2 started with breakfast at the Bai Hotel Cafe Buffet. After breakfast, we boarded a bus and went to Dynata Philippines Inc., where the team discussed workplace safety, hazard management, and proper procedures to ensure a secure and efficient work environment. After the visit, we had lunch at Buffet 101 and then proceeded to Rivant IT Cebu. There, we learned about IT-related jobs, where to find them, and how to acquire the necessary skills. They also provided a hands-on session on CISCO technologies, giving us practical insights into networking and IT systems. We ended the day by returning to Bai Hotel to rest and prepare for the following day of our educational tour.

Day 3

On Day 3, we started the day with breakfast at the Bai Hotel Cafe Buffet. After breakfast, we checked out and boarded the bus, heading to MATA Technologies. There, we were introduced to the virtual maps they develop, had the opportunity to experience virtual reality through VR goggles, and learned more about the innovative projects and technologies the company is working on. After the visit, we had lunch at Somac Korean Buffet at SM Seaside Cebu, followed by some free time to explore and roam around the mall. Later, we boarded the bus again and proceeded to the pier to catch the SuperCat ferry to Tagbilaran. The ferry ride took around three hours, after which we arrived in Tagbilaran. From there, we boarded a bus and headed to Panglao, where we had dinner before checking in at Panglao Vista Suites to rest for the night.

Day 4

On Day 4, we had breakfast at Vista Suites, checked out, and boarded a bus to visit T.A.R.S.I.E.R. 117. They discussed their role as Bohol’s emergency hotline and disaster management system, explaining how it coordinates police, fire, and medical responses, the history of "117" before the national 911 switch, and how the tarsier symbolizes alertness and quick, reliable help. Afterward, we explored Bohol, including the Mirror of the World, where calm waters reflect the surrounding landscape. We did some pasalubong shopping before lunch at Fiesta Buffet at the Floating Restaurant while cruising the Loboc River with Harana performances. In the afternoon, we visited the Man-Made Forest, Tarsier Conservation Area, and the Chocolate Hills. Dinner was at South Farm Panglao, then we proceeded to Panglao Airport for our flight to Manila. After a brief layover at NAIA, we boarded our flight to Davao, concluding our educational tour.
